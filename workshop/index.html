~ talk title

Talking to (and hacking) the logic of the blockchain world!

~ talk sypnosis

With the rise of buzzwords like Web3, blockchain, smart contracts, and NFTs, have you ever wondered what the technology behind them is and how it works?

In this workshop, we'll explore how the blockchain (or more specifically, Smart Contracts) functions and how we can interact with it.

Finally, we'll explore some common vulnerabilities in Smart Contracts that hackers may exploit to access large sums of money.

~ requirements

Bring a laptop, with the metamask browser extension installed!

~ about speaker

Elma is a second-year Information Security student at the National University of Singapore (NUS) and a member of the NUS Greyhats club. He is an avid cybersecurity and CTF enthusiast, constantly exploring and learning new things, particularly in vulnerability research and reverse engineering.
